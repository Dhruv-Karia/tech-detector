In the modern era, our world is dominated by technology. The rise of the internet made our lives more integrated than ever with the so-called “information superhighway.” However, this has had its downsides. Student engagement is often unreliable, as many students will sneak in phones or tablets. Airport security agents have to identify technology quickly in a time-sensitive environment, and millions of tons of electronic waste is generated each year, a lot of it still usable. What if an AI could help solve these problems? That's why we made this. This is the AI Detechtor. An object detection AI, with amazing potential. A tool that could prove very valuable for schools, Airports, and everyday activities. Our AI is able to detect 6 forms of common technology. Phones, laptops, headphones, keyboards, tablets, and game controllers.
